Appointments

Use the Book Appointment form on our website, call our appointment desk at +91 422 456 7890, or walk in during OPD hours. Online requests are confirmed by phone within the hour.

Billing & Insurance

We accept most major insurance providers and offer cashless treatment for empanelled insurers. Bring your insurance card and ID to the billing counter on admission.

Yes, our billing counsellor provides a written estimate before any planned procedure or admission, so there are no surprises.

Visiting & OPD

General wards: 11 AM–1 PM and 5 PM–7 PM daily. ICU visiting is limited to 15 minutes, twice a day, for immediate family only.

Most departments run Monday–Saturday, 8 AM–8 PM. Individual specialist timings are listed on each department's page.

Emergency

Call 1066 or 100 for our 24×7 emergency helpline and ambulance dispatch. Our Emergency Block is open every day, all day.

Ambulance dispatch within city limits is free for genuine emergencies. Longer-distance transfers are charged at standard mileage rates.
