Our story

27 years of care, one mission

From a 30-bed nursing home in 1998 to a 220-bed multispecialty hospital today.

Our history

Built by doctors who wanted to stay close to their patients

Sanjeevani began in 1998 as a small nursing home founded by Dr. K. Sanjeevan, a general physician who believed Coimbatore needed accessible, honest healthcare. What started with 30 beds and three departments has grown into a 220-bed multispecialty hospital with 13 departments and a Level II trauma centre — without losing the personal attention it was built on.

1998

A 30-bed nursing home opens

General medicine and maternity care for the local community.

2006

First major expansion

Cardiology, orthopedics and a modern operation theatre added.

2014

NABH accreditation

Recognised for quality and patient-safety standards.

2026

220-bed multispecialty hospital

13 departments, ICU, robotic surgery suite and a 24×7 trauma centre.

What drives us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver safe, evidence-based healthcare that is accessible to every family in the region, regardless of their ability to pay, and to treat every patient with the dignity we'd want for our own.

Our Vision

To be South India's most trusted community hospital — recognised as much for clinical outcomes as for the way patients are made to feel while achieving them.

Leadership

A message from our Chairman

"When we started in 1998, our only goal was to make sure no one in our community had to travel to another city for good treatment. Today, as we cross 27 years, that goal hasn't changed — only our ability to deliver on it has grown. Every expansion we've made, from our ICU to our trauma centre, has been guided by one question: does this bring us closer to our patients, or further away? I'm proud to say we've always chosen closer."

Dr. K. Sanjeevan
Chairman & Founder
